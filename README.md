# Tata Data Visualisation: Empowering Business with Effective Insights

## Overview
I am highly enthusiastic to present this data visualization project, completed as part of the Tata Virtual Experience Program on Forage. This project focuses on empowering business leadership with precise, data-driven insights. By meticulously cleaning raw retail data and translating it into compelling visual narratives, this project bridges the gap between complex datasets and strategic business decision-making, equipping the executive team with the clarity needed to identify future business opportunities.

## Problem Statement
The executive leadership required a comprehensive review of their operational and financial data to guide upcoming strategic planning. The core objectives were to identify any underlying seasonality in store sales, evaluate the performance of countries with the highest growth potential (specifically excluding the already high-demand UK market), and assess the revenue concentration among the top customer base. The challenge was to deliver a clean, error-free analysis that directly answers these guiding questions and highlights clear avenues for expansion.

## Analysis Done
To ensure the highest accuracy and deliver actionable business intelligence, a rigorous data preparation and analysis phase was conducted:

* **Data Scrubbing:** Cleansed the raw dataset by entirely removing records containing negative quantities and negative unit prices. This ensured the foundational data was error-free and reliable for executive-level review.
* **Trend & Seasonality Analysis:** Mapped out monthly revenue generation to identify seasonal spikes, calculating baseline averages for the first eight months and tracking percentage growth during the final quarter (excluding the incomplete December data).
* **Market Segmentation:** Filtered out the primary market (UK) to isolate and analyze the performance of international regions, focusing on unit volumes and total revenue generated in emerging markets.
* **Customer Concentration Analysis:** Evaluated the purchasing power of the top 10 customers to determine the company's reliance on key accounts and assess overall buyer bargaining power.

## Dashboard

> File for Charts is `Task III`

The visual presentation was systematically tailored to address the leadership's specific business questions:

* **Revenue Trend Analysis:** A time-series visual demonstrating that the first eight months maintain a steady average revenue of approximately $685k, followed by a sharp 40% increase in September, ultimately peaking at $1.5 million in November.
* **Top 10 Countries for Growth:** A targeted visual highlighting high-volume performance in the Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, and France, completely isolating these growth opportunities from the baseline UK metrics.
* **Top Customer Revenue:** A comparative chart showing a highly balanced distribution among the top 10 buyers. It visually highlights that the highest-generating customer only purchased 17% more than the second highest, indicating low reliance on single entities.
* **Global Revenue Map:** A spatial distribution map revealing a heavy concentration of sales in the European region and Australia, with minimal presence in the Americas and a complete absence of demand in Africa, Asia, and Russia.

## Recommendations
Based on the robust visual analysis and data scrubbing, the following strategic actions are highly recommended to the leadership team to maximize profitability:

* **Capitalize on Q4 Seasonality:** The data clearly indicates a strong seasonal surge starting in September and peaking in November. The business should proactively align its inventory, marketing budgets, and promotional efforts to capture maximum value during these critical months.
* **Deepen Penetration in Secondary Markets:** With the UK already experiencing saturated demand, targeted investments and localized marketing campaigns should be directed toward the Netherlands, Ireland, Germany, France, and Australia to capture an even larger share of these proven, high-potential regions.
* **Explore Untapped Global Territories:** The geographic analysis highlights a massive strategic gap in the Americas, Africa, Asia, and Russia. Developing a new market entry strategy targeting these specific areas presents a significant opportunity to boost overall global sales revenues.
* **Maintain Broad Customer Acquisition:** The current low bargaining power of buyers places the business in a highly secure position. The company should continue its broad acquisition strategy to ensure revenue generation remains diversified and never overly reliant on a small cluster of top-tier clients.
